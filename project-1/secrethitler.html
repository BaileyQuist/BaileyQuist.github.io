Taking place during the Reichstag of the Weimar Republic, “Secret Hitler” is a tense and often serious game where the hidden roles divide players into two teams: the liberals and the fascists. Known only to the fascists, one of their teammates is secretly Hitler. However, Hitler does not know who his fascists are, not do any of the other players know who is on their team. Players take turns attempting to enact policies related to their team while the fascists try to get Hitler elected as chancellor and the liberals attempt to assassinate him.

Players: 5-10

Play time: 30-60 minutes
