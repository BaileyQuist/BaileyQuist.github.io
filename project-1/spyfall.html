In “Spyfall”, every player recieves a card with a location and their job within that location, except for one player who’s card simply says “spy”. Players take turns asking each other questions related to the shared location, but the questions must be carefully worded, because while the players are trying to reveal the spy, it’s the spy’s job to guess the location they are in. Whoever makes their guess correctly first wins!

Players: 3-8

Play time: 15 minutes
