Hidden Role Games

Hidden role games, also called social deduction games, rely on deception and deduction in order to win. These games operate by giving one or more players a secret role with its own special objective that must be met for that player to win the game. Since hidden role games are often centered around good vs. evil, lying can be crucial to a successful and suspenseful game!
