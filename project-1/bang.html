“BANG” groups players agains each other in the style of a spaghetti western. Every player gets a character card that gives them a special ability, and a secret role card: sheriff, deputy, outlaw, and renegade. The sheriff is revealed at the top of the game and it’s their and the deputy’s job to get rid of all the secret outlaws and the renegade. The outlaws win by getting rid of everyone else, and the renegade wins as long as the sheriff is dead. Load up your six shooters and head to the town square because this is the wild wild west of card games!

Players: 4-7

Play time: 20-40 minutes
