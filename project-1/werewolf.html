Bezier Games’ “One Night Ultimate Werewolf” is a fast-paced, modular card game where every player is dealt a secret role. The goal of the game is to figure out who the werewolf is in just one round. Each role gives the player special abilities and objectives that may help them discover who the werewolf is, or perhaps aid the werewolf in staying hidden and winning. “One Night Ultimate Werewolf” can be paired with Bezier’s other “One Night” series games such as “One Night Ultimate Alien”, “One Night Ultimate Vampire”, and the “One Night Ultimate Bonus Roles”.

Players: 3-10

Play time: 10 minutes
